**Before start flashing.....**
- Always have backup of your internal storage

----

**Clean flash:**
- Download the ROM
- fastboot boot [boot](https://dl.twrp.me/fogos/twrp-3.7.1_12-0-fogos.img)
- Reboot to Recovery
- Wipe Dalvik/cache and data
- Flash the ROM
- Tap on wipe data, type yes and reboot

**Dirty flash:**
- Download the ROM
- fastboot boot [boot](https://dl.twrp.me/fogos/twrp-3.7.1_12-0-fogos.img)
- Reboot to Recovery
- Flash the ROM
- Reboot and enjoy
